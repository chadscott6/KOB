King of the Beach

An invite-only doubles tournament using King of the Court rules: defend the king side to score, or challenge your way onto it.

Request a spot View details Rulebook
Teams Fixed pairs

Each team has two players. No rotating partners.

Scoring King side only

Points can only be earned while your team is on the king side.

Flow Challenge to score

Challengers serve. Win the rally as challenger and you take the king side.

Rounds Timed elimination

Multiple teams enter each match. Low teams drop after each round.

Level Competitive

Bring clean touches, smart shots, and enough legs for a long day.
